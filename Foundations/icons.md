# Icons

## HIG Recommendation
- Create unique and recognizable icons using SF Symbols when appropriate.
- Symmetrical padding is not always the best choice, especially for asymmetrical icons. Optical alignment often creates a more balanced appearance.
- Provide a selected state for icons, such as in toolbars or tab bars.
- Always provide accessibility descriptions and alternative text labels.
- Avoid using replicas of Apple hardware products because their designs change frequently.
