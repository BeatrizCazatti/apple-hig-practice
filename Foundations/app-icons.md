# App icons

- A unique and memorable icon expresses personality and purpose.
- You can use (Icon Composer)[!https://developer.apple.com/documentation/Xcode/creating-your-app-icon-using-icon-composer] to create and prewie your app icon.

#### Best practices
- Simple icons are easier to recognize quickly, scale better, and remain memorable across contexts.
- Prefer clearly defined edges in foreground layers.
- Vary the opacity in foreground layers to increase the sense of depth and liveliness.
- Design a background that both stands out and emphasizes foreground content.
- Let the system handle blurring and other **visual effects**.
- Keep your icon’s features consistent across **appearances**.
- Consider offering alternate app icons in iOS, iPadOS, tvOS, visionOS, since they can have different layouts, styles, shapes and apperances.
